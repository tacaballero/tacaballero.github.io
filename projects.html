Slavery Awareness Fair

Throughout my sophomore year, the entirety of my class at Excelsior created and organized a Slavery Awareness Fair. The sole purpose of this event was to inform the public about the dangers and existence of modern day slavery. My contribution to the fair involved creating a website to publicize the event as well as further inform the public. I served as a project manager throughout the process and oversaw, editted, and created content and design plans for the webpage.

P-TECH Film Challenge

As a part of the P-TECH film club, I aided the creation of a short film regarding the purpose and importance of our program. The movie specifically outlines why Excelsior Academy is different from a regular high school as well as other P-TECH schools.
